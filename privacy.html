Privacy Policy

At Map2Map™, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  1. Business information (name, address, contact details)
  2. Google account access (with user consent) to manage Google Business Profile
  3. Browsing data via cookies and analytics tools

2. How We Use Your Information

  1. To deliver and improve our Google Maps management services
  2. To communicate updates, service improvements, and support
  3. To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

  1. We do not sell your data.
  2. Data is shared only with trusted third-party tools strictly necessary to deliver our services (e.g., Google APIs).

4. Your Choices

  1. You can withdraw consent for data use related to your Google account at any time.
  2. Contact us to request data deletion.

5. Security

  1. We employ standard security measures (SSL, access controls, data minimization).

6. Contact

  1. For privacy concerns: admin@map2map.com
